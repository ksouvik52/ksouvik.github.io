About Me

Hi! I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in Electrical & Computer Engineering at University of Southern California working under the supervision of Prof. Massoud Pedram (SPORT Lab) and Prof. Peter A. Beerel (HAL@usc) . I am an Indian by birth. My current highest academic qualification is M.Tech in Electronics (VLSI) Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. My technical interests include hardware friendly Computer vision, hardware accelerator architecture design for neural networks, in/near memory computing, computer architecture. I am extremely fond of long drive along pacific highway, traveling, playing and watching soccer and photography.
